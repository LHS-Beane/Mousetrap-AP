Course Overview

AP Physics 1 is an algebra-based, introductory college-level physics course. Students cultivate their understanding of physics through inquiry-based investigations as they explore these topics. The course is built around "Big Ideas" which provide a broad way of thinking about the physical world:

  • Systems: Objects and internal structures.
  • Fields: How objects interact over distances.
  • Force Interactions: Changes in an object's motion.
  • Conservation: Fundamental laws of mass, energy, and momentum.
