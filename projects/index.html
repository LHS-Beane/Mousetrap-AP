Engineering Challenges

Apply your theoretical knowledge to build, test, and refine real-world prototypes. The physics is in the fabrication.

The Design Process

These major projects require you to move beyond plug-and-chug equations. You will follow the engineering design cycle: Ask, Imagine, Plan, Create, and Improve. Success is measured not just by the final result, but by the data collected and the iterations made along the way.
